A.1.Rainbow

2.Describe the phenomenon in at least 100 words.
A rainbow is multicolored arc, it is made by light striking water droplets, the most usual type of a rainbow is produced when sunlight hits raindrops in front of the viewer at a precise angle(42degree).Rainbows can also be viewed around fogs ,sea spray, waterfalls.
A rainbow is an optical illusion it doesn't exist in a specific spot in the sky . The appearance of a rainbow depends on where you're standing and where the sun(or the source of light) is shining.A rainbow shows up as a spectrum of light: a band of familiar colors that include red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and violet.
